Adyar Store Visit Report

Store visited by Bharath

Visit Date: 28th October 2025

Report Summary

Visit Overview: The store visit was conducted to get the glimpse of the brand and the current VM practices.

Key Observations: The store demonstrates strong potential with several areas performing well. Customer engagement is positive, and staff members show good product knowledge. However, there are opportunities for enhancement in visual merchandising consistency especially with the visual inconsistent, lighting, the eye test locations layout & campaign pitch/ story telling.

Overall Assessment: The visit revealed both strengths and areas requiring attention. Immediate action on the identified improvement areas will significantly enhance store performance and customer satisfaction.

Detailed Analysis

1
Facade
Observation: The facade looks good, welcoming, good parking space, the flagpole signage is also good - but both of them are dark.

Recommendation: The lighting of the store which looks to be at 3500 kelvin is screaming premiumness but if it can be increased to 5500 kelvin it would make the store look more inviting from the outside.
2
Wall Bay's
Observation: The Wall bay is not functional the header should have visuals to tell a story about the category.

Recommendation: Visuals should tell and elevate the brands, currently the lightbox which has to be translit is not functional, will recommend having a greyback vinyl wrap at the visual lightbox area.

Notes: Visuals are just reference, Warli collection KV is skewed as couldn't find a readily available KV.
3
Peepstar Campaign
Observation: Kids category distinction is not clear, the peepstar campaign is not visible from the entrance, the kids section is also not defined properly.

Recommendation: Liked the current peepstar campaign but the visibility is low, will recommend having a more vinyl distinction and covering at the front to make it more visible, also the visual to elevate the look overall. Notes: Peepstar at the bottom is a reference and more customized branding based on the brand color can be picked up.
4
Eye test section
Observation: The eye test section is wide open and there is no distinction from the main sales floor, also there is no branding or signage to indicate free eye test.

Recommendation: There can be a Partition covering the Eye test section and the wall can have two additional wall bays to merchandise more products and also a signage on the door of the eye test or the door of the eye test can be changed to a visual branding of free eye test.
5
Cash Counter
Observation: Cash Counter looks spacious but empty and there is no recall of the brand with any memorable visuals for the customers to engage with while waiting at the counter.

Recommendation: they KV of major campaigns can be placed at the back wall of the cash counter to elevate the brand presence and recall also logo at the cash counter can be added to make it look complete.
6
Other feedbacks
Notes on Powered Sunglasses section: Covered in detail on VM implementation for this section.
Notes on Window back area: Window back area is a key real estiate space the campaign glass sticker on the back side is empty. Recommendation: front side visual can be replicated in the back side without the clear vinyl part, just the astralis visual.
