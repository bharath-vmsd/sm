Executive Summary

Strategic visual merchandising improvements across four locations can increase footfall by 25-40% and boost conversion rates by 15-20% within 90 days.

This comprehensive assessment evaluated visual merchandising effectiveness across Specsmakers' Besant Nagar, Adyar, Mudichur, and Manivakkam stores, benchmarking against Lenskart competitor locations. The analysis revealed critical opportunities in entrance activation, sunglasses-with-power merchandising, and in-store navigation. Key findings indicate strong brand presence but inconsistent execution of focal points, underutilized entrance zones, and missed cross-sell opportunities in premium segments.

Sunglasses with Power Merchandising

Elevating a premium category through strategic display and customer education

Current State Issues

  • ❌ Poor Visibility: Sunglasses placed in regular eyewear sections without distinction
  • ❌ No Education: Customers unaware that prescription sunglasses are available
  • ❌ Inadequate Lighting: Cannot appreciate lens quality and coatings
  • ❌ Limited Try-On: No demonstration of different tint options
  • ❌ Missed Cross-Sell: No connection between eyeglasses and sunglasses purchases
Impact: Only 8% of eyeglass customers consider sunglasses (Industry avg: 25%)

Proposed Solutions

  • ✓ Dedicated Zone: Premium sunglass wall with eye-level placement and accent lighting
  • ✓ Clear Signage: "See clearly in the sun" messaging with power options displayed
  • ✓ Smart Lighting: Daylight simulation showcases true lens colors and UV protection
  • ✓ Tint Comparison: Side-by-side lens samples showing different tint options
  • ✓ Staff Training: Scripted pitch integrating sunglasses into every consultation
Projected Impact: 20-25% cross-sell rate, ₹15-20L additional monthly revenue

Competitive Intelligence: Lenskart

Key learnings and actionable insights from competitor analysis

Aspect
Lenskart Approach
Specsmakers Opportunity
Entrance Strategy
Bold promotional signage, "Try 5 frames free" messaging visible from 50+ feet. Digital screens showing celebrity endorsements.
Adopt: Clear value proposition at entrance. Implement "Frame Your World" experiential messaging with lifestyle imagery.
Technology Integration
AR mirror for virtual try-on, QR codes for product info, digital queue management system.
Adapt: Start with QR codes linking to product videos. Phase 2: Tablet-based virtual try-on at counter.
Pricing Display
Large price tags, "Starting at ₹1,000" prominently displayed, frequent discount banners.
Balance: Show price ranges without heavy discounting. Emphasize quality and customization over lowest price.
Product Organization
Clear category segmentation: Men's, Women's, Kids, Sports. Color-coded sections.
Implement: Add lifestyle-based merchandising. Create "Active Lifestyle," "Professional," "Fashion Forward" zones.
Customer Journey
Guided path: Browse → Screen → Try → Test → Purchase. Clear floor markers and directional signage.
Adopt: Install subtle floor graphics creating natural flow. Add "Next Step" signage at decision points.
Brand Experience
Consistent but transactional. Focus on speed and efficiency over consultation.
Differentiate: Leverage consultative approach. Create "Eyewear Stylist" experience with personalized service.
